The Apartment Cafe (Ho Chi Minh)

In the bustling city of Ho Chi Minh Within district 1, there lies the famous Apartment Cafe! An apartment filled to the brim with different local cafes and boutique, all with their own unique styles. If you're looking for something hip and trendy, this is the perfect place, especially for photos! This apartment stands at a whopping height of 9 stories and due to its architecture, the viewing experience is breathtaking. As new as this sounds, the apartment has dated back to the late 1950s and was originally built as dorms for the government for the Vietnam war, it wasn't till 2010 the residents of the apartment began to capitalize on the idea of building independent business within their homes.

Pros

  • Supporting local business
  • Very aesthetics appealing, along with amazing views
  • Its unique structure along with its historic values as a building

Cons

  • Throughout the hallway, like the rest of Vietnam, it is HOT, unless your in a shop, there is no air condition
  • The elevator does cost money to ride either keep this in mind of your budget or feel free to use the stairs!
  • The prices are tourism prices, higher than your average local price

Sun World Ba Na Hill (Da Nang)

20km away from the central city of Vietnam Da Nang, rests Vietnam's leading amusement park, Sun World Ba Na Hill. Stretching over 214 hectares, this park has even more expansion plans for the future. To access the park you must ride a cable car in which the park has earned a Guinness record of the longest non-stop single cable car ride. Enjoy the beautiful views as you reach higher into the mountains.

Golden Bridge
