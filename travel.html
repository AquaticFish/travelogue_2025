Here are some places that I enjoyed visting Vietnam of 2025

Sun World Ba Na Hill (Da Nang)

20km away from the central city of Vietnam Da Nang, rests Vietnam's leading amusement park, Sun World Ba Na Hill. Stretching over 214 hectares, this park has even more expansion plans for the future. To access the park you must ride a cable car in which the park has earned a Guinness record of the longest non-stop single cable car ride.

Enjoy the beautiful views as you reach higher into the mountains. Take in the beatuy, by riding down the alpine coaster with a friend or walk around Freach inspred architecture.

Golden Bridge

Walk above a height of 1,414m the famous Golden Bridge rests high towering over the edge of the mountain. The bridge, looping above a pair of stone hands.

Popmart Castle

Popmart is a franchise known for selling the hit crazy blindboxes, well they have their own store, but a castle version!

Pros

  • Lots of things to do for all age
  • Great food
  • Its unique structure brings you to whole another country

Cons

  • There is A LOT of people there so it’s pretty busy (Long lines)
  • Can be overwhelming due to it large amount of actives

Sandy Desert (Binh Thuan)

Do you love the thrill of a speeding car? Well, if you ever thought of doing it in a desert! Welcome to Binh Thuan, where you can visit a small desert in which you can book a car or truck to ride across sandy land over bumps and hills and feel the wind through your hair. They even stop to let you ride down the sandy hill with a sheet of plastic!

If this is not your cup of tea, how about animals? Ever wanted to take a picture of a camel? How about a hawk? Maybe even ride an ostrich! Adventure awaits!

Pros

  • Amazing views, perfect for photos
  • Fantastic services
  • Super thrilling

Cons

  • It is a bit clear when to the care of the animal theres a case of abuse and can be harming to guest
  • You will get sand on you
  • The sand gets super hot during noon so be careful

The Apartment Cafe (Ho Chi Minh)

In the bustling city of Ho Chi Minh Within district 1, there lies the famous Apartment Cafe! An apartment filled to the brim with different local cafes and boutique, all with their own unique styles. If you're looking for something hip and trendy, this is the perfect place, especially for photos!

This apartment stands at a whopping height of 9 stories and due to its architecture, the viewing experience is breathtaking while dining. Enjoy taking photos as theirs lot's of artwork and photobooth on every level. Or enjoy the cozy vibe of the different cafe with lot's of fun to eat, with english friendly menu available!

As new as this sounds, the apartment has dated back to the late 1950s and was originally built as dorms for the government for the Vietnam war, it wasn't till 2010 the residents of the apartment began to capitalize on the idea of building independent business within their homes.

Pros

  • Lots of things to do for all age
  • Great food
  • Its unique structure brings you to whole another country

Cons

  • There is A LOT of people there so it’s pretty busy (Long lines)
  • Can be overwhelming due to it large amount of actives
